Case Study

The European Union has a strategy encouraging the use of open source software across the organisation. Since then, they've published a comprehensive case study on the impact of open source in the EU as a public good. Although the strategy is targeted towards governance, the effects have been felt by every day people living in the EU, for example, the usage of open source office software in schools.

Most Effective

Raising awareness, advocating for, and encouraging the usage of open source software is the single best way to increase usage, because everyday people generally just lack the awareness or motivation.

Use of Technology

Social media can provide a means of spreading information to everyday people, and thus can be used to spread awareness and encourage usage of open source software.

Implication

As a supplement to social media, a beautiful, simple to navigate gallery showcasing the best of open source software can be used to improve perception and encourage adoption.
