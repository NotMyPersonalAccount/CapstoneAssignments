Research Question

How can we increase adoption of free open source software?

Context

Proprietary software, as opposed to open software, is very commonly used by everyday people. This software does not allow for the user to know fully what it is doing or to maintain it, resulting in full reliance on a company.

Open-source software adoption is rather low in many cases despite alternatives existing.

Solution

Through the use of social media, and nice-looking websites to showcase open-source software, awareness and perception can be improved.
